Dimensiva - 3D Camera

Dimensiva - 3D Camera enables you to make stereoscopic 3D photos with your iPhone. Use Dimensiva to realize your great ideas and get fast and reliable results with Dimensivas intuitively user interface.

iPhone camera for stereoscopy

The 3D photos gives a great impression on 3D TVs. View photos directly on your 3D TV via Airplay®! Or simply use the special views (parallel, cross, anaglyph) on the iPhone to get a 3D effect. Try a Google Cardboard! Photos from the Dimensiva gallery or the integrated galleries Phereo and Flickr give you an impression what is possible.

Show your 3D photos to your friends

Use the Dimensiva gallery to share photos with other Dimensiva users. Send photos by email, facebook, or as a message. Or simply upload your photos to your Dropbox folder.
